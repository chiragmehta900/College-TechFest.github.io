Poster Competition

Department : General Events(Technical)

Team Members : Maximum 4 person and minimum 3 person can participate.

Fees per Student : ₹ 50 Per Person

Event Description :

  • A poster presentation, at a congress or conference with an academic or professional focus, is the presentation of research information in the form of a paper poster that conference participants may view. Quite often, poster content is embargoed from release to the public until the commencement of the poster session.
  • Define the purpose.
  • Minimum two student and maximum five student.
  • The title is important.
  • Many of the rules for writing a good paper apply to poster.
  • Good poster have unique features.
  • Layout and formate are critical.
